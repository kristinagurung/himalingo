When i used to listen or generally when i hear people conversing in Lhokay it just sounds so pleasant and beautiful. But i always put off learning for the longest time. Considering that we( especially the youngsters) are always on our phones I felt it would be more effective if this course could be accessed on our phones directly. So i took up this opportunity to put up a simple course on Lhokay. This is a minor,hopefully a fruitful attempt ?,on my part in trying to engage people(especially) our kids to learn our slowly but surely fading language.The course has been curated in a way or from a point of view of a complete beginner so that absolute beginners dont get too overwhelmed and stop learning halfway.
